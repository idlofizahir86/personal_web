LuminaSense - Capstone Bangkit 2024

LuminaSense is an advanced smart lighting and human presence monitoring system designed to enhance energy efficiency and security in modern homes and businesses. Integrating AI and IoT technologies, LuminaSense provides real-time live camera feeds, supports various camera models, and utilizes AI-based human detection to accurately monitor occupancy and optimize lighting...

Key Features:
  • Integrated Camera Devices
  • Real-Time Live Camera Feed
  • Human Detection Capability
Benefits:
  • Energy Efficiency
  • Enhanced Security
  • User-Friendly Interface
Why LuminaSense?

LuminaSense is not just about smart lighting; it's about creating a smarter, safer, and more energy-efficient environment for everyone.
